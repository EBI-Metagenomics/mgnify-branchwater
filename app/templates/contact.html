MGnify: Metagenomics Analysis and Data

This instance of Branchwater is run by the MGnify team at EMBL-EBI.
MGnify is a bioinformatics resource that offers an automated pipeline for the analysis and archiving of metagenomic and metatranscriptomic data. It's part of the European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI). MGnify provides insights into the diversity and function of microbial communities across a range of environments, including oceans, soil, organisms, and the human body. It's a valuable tool for researchers studying microbiomes and metagenomes.
